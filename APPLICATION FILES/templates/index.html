F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the average rainfall recorded in various states in India?

    This is done with the help of a map shown in the above visualizations.

  • What is the amount of rainfall recorded in different years from 1901-2015?

    This is implemented using line chart which is shown in the above visualizations.

  • What are the states having high Rainfall?

    This is shown with the help of horizontal bar chart and also used a parameter which gives the TOP N states of highest rainfall .

  • what is the rainfall recorded in different states in particular season?

    This is shown with the help of bar chart with a parameter of season.We can select the season what we want from the parameter and we get the analysis based on that in the form of a horizontal bar chart.

  • Which season had the most rainfall in all years ranging from 1901-2015?

    This is done with the help of a density chart which gives us an insight of the season having highest rainfall.From the visualization above we can say that the season of JUN-SEP is having highest rainfall.

  • What are the states having least Rainfall?

    This is shown with the help of horizontal bar chart and also used a parameter which gives the BOTTOM N states of least rainfall .

  • what is the rainfall recorded in different states in all seasons?

    This is shown with the help of heat map and also used a parameter of SEASONS from where we can select the season that we want.
